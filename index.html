About

I like to take part in new projects that involve new technologies and solutions. I have a lot of experience in creating functional and demanding applications based on limited hardware resources. I am not afraid of new challenges and imposed limitations resulting from security or the final price of the product.

I have a broad knowledge of electronics and design principles. In my work I often use solutions based on other programming languages to speed up the creation process.
